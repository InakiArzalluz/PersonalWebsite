MKV Downmixer

Easy to use python app that automatically batch-converts MKV-based media files from 5.1 surround sound to stereo, while preserving voice clarity.

The solution leverages the power of FFmpeg, ffprobe, and mkvmerge to ensure reliable audio extraction, conversion, and remuxing, while providing multiple downmix algorithms to better fit the user’s needs.
